Trend Discovered Between Obesity and Poverty

U.S. Census Report by State

Latest research from the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System indicates a startling set of findings. Across the United States, as the poverty rate increases, there is a higher percentage of obesity. There is a growing concern over the lack of fresh produce in communities with limited or subsidized housing.

Source: U.S. Census Bureau and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System
